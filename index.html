About Me

Tinkering and deconstructing things has always been a side hobby of mine. Car parts, electronics, machinery, and tools are a few things I’ve ripped apart to fix and then rebuilt to working order. The code languages I have been learning and reverse engineering have felt about the same. Tearing things down to their basic properties and figuring out how each piece fits back together. If things don’t fit correctly the whole thing won’t work. Some pieces take trial and error to figure out where exactly they fit in the puzzle. Breaking things down into steps and creating a plan of action is important with car parts as it is with setting up flexbox on a webpage. This hobby has now been channeled into a career building skill and I look forward to all I can create. Below are some of my projects.
